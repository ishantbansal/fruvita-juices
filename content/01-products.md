## FRUVITA ORIGINALS

### 1. Mango Juice
**Tagline:** "Sunshine in a Bottle"
**Short:** Made from ripe, fibrous Alphonso-style mangoes, thick and golden — the taste of peak summer, any day of the year.
**Long:** Fruvita Mango is built around one idea: the mango should taste like the mango, not like mango-flavored syrup. We use ripe, naturally sweet mangoes for a thick, golden juice with real pulp body. Mangoes are rich in vitamin C and vitamin A, supporting immunity and skin health.
**Image direction:** Ripe mangoes, one halved to show flesh, condensation on a poured glass, warm golden light.

### 2. Guava Juice
**Tagline:** "The Everyday Superfruit"
**Short:** Pink-fleshed guava, naturally tart-sweet, and packed with more vitamin C than an orange.
**Long:** Guava is one of the most nutrient-dense fruits around — a single serving of guava juice can carry several times the vitamin C of orange juice, plus potassium and dietary fiber. Fruvita Guava keeps that natural tang intact instead of masking it with added sugar.
**Health fact:** Guava juice can contain 200%+ of daily vitamin C value per serving — several times more than orange juice.
**Image direction:** Pink guava halves, seeds visible, deep pink-toned juice in a clear glass.

### 3. Orange Juice
**Tagline:** "Squeezed, Not Guessed"
**Short:** Classic, tangy-sweet orange juice — no shortcuts, just real oranges.
**Long:** The one everybody knows and nobody should compromise on. Fruvita Orange is bright, tangy, and unmistakably real — a breakfast-table staple built for consistency, not gimmicks.
**Image direction:** Halved oranges, juice mid-pour, bright citrus-yellow backdrop.

### 4. Lychee (Litchi) Juice
**Tagline:** "Delicately Sweet, Deeply Refreshing"
**Short:** Fragrant, floral, and lightly sweet — lychee juice is the quiet luxury of the Fruvita lineup.
**Long:** Litchi is prized for its delicate floral sweetness and is rich in vitamin C and polyphenol antioxidants — in fact, lychee carries more polyphenols than many common fruits like apples. Fruvita Litchi captures that fragrance in a lightly sweet, refreshing juice.
**Health fact:** Lychee is high in polyphenol antioxidants (e.g. epicatechin, rutin) linked to heart health and reduced inflammation.
**Image direction:** Peeled lychees showing translucent flesh, husks scattered artfully, pale blush-pink juice.

### 5. Pineapple Juice
**Tagline:** "Tropical, Uncut"
**Short:** Bright, tangy pineapple juice with real tropical bite.
**Long:** Fruvita Pineapple brings the tang and brightness of fresh-cut pineapple, balancing sweetness with the fruit's natural acidity for a juice that wakes up the palate.
**Image direction:** Pineapple crown and cut rings, bright yellow juice, tropical leaf backdrop.

### 6. Pomegranate Juice
**Tagline:** "Bold. Ruby Red. Real."
**Short:** Deep, ruby-red pomegranate juice with a rich, slightly tart edge.
**Long:** Pomegranate has long been associated with heart health thanks to its antioxidant-rich profile. Fruvita Pomegranate is deep red, robust, and unapologetically bold — a juice for people who want flavor with substance.
**Image direction:** Split-open pomegranate with arils spilling out, deep red juice, dark moody backdrop for contrast.

### 7. Apple Drink
**Tagline:** "Crisp, Clean, Classic"
**Short:** A crisp, lightly sweet apple drink — simple, familiar, and universally loved.
**Long:** Sometimes the classics are classics for a reason. Fruvita Apple Drink is clean, crisp, and easy-drinking — a dependable choice for kids and adults alike.
**Image direction:** Fresh red/green apples, one sliced, pale golden juice.

### 8. Peachy Juice
**Tagline:** "Soft, Sweet, Sun-Ripened"
**Short:** Smooth, fragrant peach juice with a naturally soft sweetness.
**Long:** Fruvita Peachy Juice is built around the fruit's natural, mellow sweetness and fragrance — a softer, rounder flavor profile than the citrus and tropical fruits in the lineup.
**Image direction:** Halved peaches showing the stone, fuzzy skin texture visible, soft warm lighting.

## FRUVITA MIX & THRILL (bold / blended / novelty line)

### 9. Mixed Fruit Juice
**Tagline:** "Everything Good, In One Glass"
**Short:** A blend of orchard favorites in one bottle — balanced, never one-note.
**Long:** Can't pick just one? Fruvita Mixed Fruit blends multiple real fruits (mango, apple, orange and more) into a single balanced juice — layered flavor without a dominant, overpowering note.
**Image direction:** A flat-lay of multiple whole fruits (mango, apple, orange, grapes) around a poured glass.

### 10. Cranberry Thrill
**Tagline:** "Tart Meets Bold"
**Short:** Sharp, tart cranberry juice with a bold, grown-up edge.
**Long:** Cranberries bring a distinctive tartness that's less "sweet treat," more "wake-up call." Fruvita Cranberry Thrill is for the drinker who wants flavor with a bit of an edge — and cranberries are well known for supporting urinary tract and antioxidant health.
**Image direction:** Scattered fresh cranberries, deep red juice, cool-toned lighting to emphasize "thrill"/bold positioning.

### 11. Guava Thrill
**Tagline:** "Guava, Turned Up"
**Short:** A bolder, sweeter spin on classic guava — for the Thrill line's fans of intensity.
**Long:** Where Fruvita Guava Juice plays it classic, Guava Thrill turns up the intensity — a punchier, sweeter guava experience positioned for a younger, bolder-flavor audience.
**Image direction:** Similar to Guava Juice but styled with brighter, more saturated color grading and dynamic splash photography.

### 12. Kiwi Kiss
**Tagline:** "A Little Tangy, A Little Sweet"
**Short:** Bright green kiwi juice — tangy, sweet, and refreshingly different.
**Long:** Kiwi is an underused hero: tangy-sweet, vitamin C-rich, and visually striking with its green flesh and black seeds. Fruvita Kiwi Kiss brings something different to the shelf.
**Image direction:** Halved kiwis showing green flesh and seed pattern, vivid green juice.

### 13. Electric Peachy Drink
**Tagline:** "Peach, With a Buzz"
**Short:** A brighter, fizzier take on peach — bold color, bold flavor.
**Long:** The "Electric" sub-variant of peach is built for standout shelf presence and a punchier flavor profile than the classic Peachy Juice — positioned as a fun, energetic alternative.
**Image direction:** Vivid orange-pink color grading, dynamic splash/motion shot, bold graphic background.
