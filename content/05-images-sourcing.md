## B. AI Image Prompts (for custom hero/product shots)
Paste these into an AI image generator. Style anchor to keep consistent across all images:

**Base style suffix to append to every prompt:**
`, natural warm lighting, shallow depth of field, clean minimal background, professional product photography style, high detail, no text, no logos`

| Flavor | Prompt |
|---|---|
| Mango | "A glass bottle of golden mango juice next to two ripe mangoes, one sliced open showing orange flesh, condensation droplets on the bottle" |
| Guava | "A glass of pink guava juice beside halved pink-fleshed guavas with visible seeds, soft pink and green tones" |
| Orange | "A glass of fresh orange juice mid-pour from a pitcher, halved oranges scattered around, bright citrus lighting" |
